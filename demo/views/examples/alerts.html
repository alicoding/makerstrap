Edit me in thimble

You create alerts with one of the colour-specific classes:

class="alert alert-success"
Well done!
class="alert alert-info"
Heads up!
class="alert alert-warning"
Warning!
class="alert alert-danger"
Oh snap!

If you want to add links within alerts then use an <a> tag and the <alert-link> class.
